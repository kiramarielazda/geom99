ABOUT THE PROJECT

The Friends of the Dumoine River volunteer organization has requested a fully open-source and dynamic web map that users can interact with to plan their hike along the newly created Dumoine River Tote Road trail. In the 1800s, a tote road ran alongside the Dumoine River, one of southern Canada’s last undammed rivers, and was home to lumberjack camps, farms and Indigenous families. Until recently, the road had been reclaimed by the forest. Current volunteer efforts are underway to create a new 26-kilometer hiking path for hardy outdoor enthusiasts to enjoy and having a web map to showcase the uniqueness of the trail will garner the attention the client needs to make this endeavor a success.

Ideal

Users can use the web map to view the full trail, get information about campsites, historical landmarks, and interesting natural and manmade feature as pop-ups on the map. Users will also have the ability to filter which layers they would like to see on the map and search for features of interest to them.

Reality

Only general reference maps currently exist for the area and additional trail data may need to be collected and transferred into a useable format. There is no tool currently available to collect POI data but can be created using open source solutions. Typically a web map for hiking trails will not have info popups and layer functionality integrated into it.

Consequences

Having to switch from looking at a trail map to reading about campsites, interesting view points or historical facts on a separate page or even separate website is disjointed and cumbersum for the user, and the overall process is less convenient for someone looking to plan their overnight or day hikes.

Proposal

Create a web map to showcase collected data, inform the public about the trail, and provide hiking and camping details. Main focus is to keep any solutions open-source. Create a form to collect data about points of interest along the trail.

Endless Possibilities, One Solution

To ensure we provide our client with the best product we can offer, we made sure to test out many possible solutions.
